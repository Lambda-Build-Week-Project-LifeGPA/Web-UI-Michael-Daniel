About LifeGPA

LifeGPA is a habit tracking application that helps you see how well you are keeping your routines. You can select what habbits to track or add your own. For every day you keep your habbit, your LifeGPA will increase. You'll get a your LifeGPA report card every 30, 60, and 90 days.
